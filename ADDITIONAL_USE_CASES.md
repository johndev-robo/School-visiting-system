# Additional Use Cases & Features for Boarding School Visiting System

## Executive Summary
The current system provides a solid foundation for visit management. By expanding into adjacent functionality, we can transform it into a comprehensive **School Management & Safety Platform** suitable for premium positioning in the East African market.

---

## 1. EMERGENCY & CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### 1.1 Emergency Lockdown Mode
**Use Case**: When security threats occur, the system can trigger an instant lockdown alert.
- **Features**:
  - All pending visits auto-cancelled with SMS notification
  - Security gates locked with status alerts
  - Admin receives real-time dashboard of who's on campus
  - Automatic incident log creation
  - One-click emergency alert to parents/guardians

**Target Value**: Schools can demonstrate rapid crisis response to parents & regulators.

### 1.2 Missing Person Protocol
**Use Case**: If a student goes missing during visiting hours.
- **Features**:
  - Quick-search interface showing which parents were on campus during incident
  - CCTV integration points (log when student was last scanned at gates)
  - Automatic timeline report generation
  - Emergency contact auto-trigger

**Target Value**: Liability reduction + duty of care demonstration.

### 1.3 Medical Emergency Integration
**Use Case**: Track student medical incidents related to visits.
- **Features**:
  - Link visiting incidents to medical log
  - Restrict visits for students with active medical leave
  - Parent medical history integration (allergies, conditions)
  - Emergency contact escalation

**Target Value**: Regulatory compliance + parent confidence.

## 2. HEALTH & SAFETY COMPLIANCE

### 2.1 Health Screening at Entry
**Use Case**: Pre-visit health declaration & temperature checks.
- **Features**:
  - Parents complete health questionnaire before approval
  - Temperature log integration (for security checkpoint)
  - Dashboard shows health status trends
  - Auto-decline visits if health flags detected

**Target Value**: Post-COVID regulatory requirement; differentiator for premium schools.

### 2.2 Visitor Behavior Scoring
**Use Case**: Track parent reputation based on past visits.
- **Features**:
  - Incident flagging (verbal warnings, boundary violations, etc.)
  - Cumulative behavior score
  - Auto-notify admin if "high-risk" parent requests visit
  - Tiered restrictions (unrestricted → supervised → banned)

**Target Value**: Safeguarding tool; reduces instances of rule violations.

### 2.3 Substance/Contraband Detection Alerts
**Use Case**: Flag suspicious visit patterns or banned items.
- **Features**:
  - System flags visits from known problematic guardians
  - Item declaration form (what parents bringing in)
  - Integration with security inspection logs
  - Auto-report to authorities if required

**Target Value**: Compliance with boarding school safeguarding standards.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL & BILLING MANAGEMENT

### 3.1 Visit Fees & Revenue Management
**Use Case**: Schools charge for special visiting sessions or extended hours.
- **Features**:
  - Tiered pricing (regular visit free, evening/weekend = fee)
  - Automated invoice generation
  - Payment gateway integration (M-Pesa, Airtel Money for Kenya)
  - Refund system for declined visits
  - Financial reporting per term/month

**Target Value**: Revenue stream; accounting automation.

### 3.2 Guardian Sponsorship & Fees
**Use Case**: Track which guardians have outstanding school fees.
- **Features**:
  - Integrate with accounting system
  - Flag accounts with overdue fees
  - Email reminder: "Complete fee payment to unlock visiting privileges"
  - Automatic visit hold if fees >30 days overdue

**Target Value**: Schools gain leverage for fee collection.

### 3.3 Donation & Payment Reconciliation
**Use Case**: Combine visiting system with donations (sports day visits, charity events).
- **Features**:
  - Event-based visiting (premium events with entry fees)
  - Donation tracking during visits
  - Automated receipts for tax purposes
  - Dashboard showing total raised per event

**Target Value**: Integrated financial tool for development offices.

## 5. REPORTING, ANALYTICS & INSIGHTS

### 5.1 Visitor Analytics Dashboard
**Use Case**: School leadership analyzes parent engagement trends.
- **Features**:
  - "Frequency of Visits" per student (identifies neglected students)
  - Peak visiting hours heatmap
  - Guardian type breakdown (parent, aunt, uncle, etc.)
  - Geographic origin map (which regions do visitors come from)
  - Comparative stats (Class 1 avg 3.2 visits/term vs Class 2: 2.8)

**Target Value**: Identifies student welfare issues early; supports interventions.

### 5.2 Staff Workload Reporting
**Use Case**: Assess security/admin personnel workload.
- **Features**:
  - Entry/exit volume per gate per hour
  - Peak congestion alerts
  - Staff overtime tracking
  - Staffing recommendations (need extra guards on Visiting Sundays?)

**Target Value**: Operational efficiency; budget justification.

### 5.3 Regulatory Compliance Reports
**Use Case**: Generate audit-ready reports for government inspections.
- **Features**:
  - "All visitors logged" report
  - Background check verification status
  - Incident summaries
  - Visit request approval rate analysis
  - Export to PDF/Excel for regulators

**Target Value**: Regulatory confidence; liability protection.

### 5.4 Longitudinal Student Wellness Analytics
**Use Case**: Track student well-being trends based on visit frequency.
- **Features**:
  - Correlate visit frequency with academic performance
  - Identify students with declining visit patterns
  - Cross-reference with incident reports (behavioral issues)
  - Generate "student at-risk" alerts for counselors

**Target Value**: Holistic student support; enables proactive intervention.

## 6. STUDENT & ACADEMIC INTEGRATION

### 6.1 Academic Progress Sharing During Visits
**Use Case**: Parents can view real-time academic updates during or after visits.
- **Features**:
  - Grade snapshot (recent test scores, assignment performance)
  - Tutor notes visible to parents post-approval
  - Attendance record (study hall participation)
  - Subject-wise performance trends
  - Upcoming assessment dates

**Target Value**: Enhanced parent-school partnership; differentiator from competitors.

### 6.2 Student Preference & Relationship Mapping
**Use Case**: Track which guardians a student wants to visit.
- **Features**:
  - Student can set preferred visitors (restrict visits from estranged parent)
  - Preference-based matching (flag request from non-preferred guardian)
  - Flag complex family situations (custody disputes)
  - Link to counselor notes (trauma-informed care)

**Target Value**: Student agency & safeguarding integration.

### 6.3 Visiting Day Educational Events
**Use Case**: Combine visiting with parent education (workshops, seminars).
- **Features**:
  - Session sign-up during visit booking (parent attends workshop on nutrition)
  - Attendance tracking for parents
  - Feedback collection
  - Certificate generation for professional development
  - Linked to school fee account (can be discounted for attendees)

**Target Value**: Enhanced parent engagement; school-family partnership metrics.

## 7. MULTI-DIMENSIONAL USER MANAGEMENT

### 7.1 Guardian Relationship Complexity

- **Features**:

  - Custody document storage (digital filing)

- **Features**:

  - Department-level restrictions (House Master sees only their house visits)
  - Time-based permissions (duty-schedule visibility)
  - Audit log of who accessed what when
  - Two-factor authentication for sensitive operations

**Target Value**: Reduces errors; protects against insider threats.

### 7.3 Alumni & Extended Community Visits
**Use Case**: Manage visits from alumni and school supporters.
- **Features**:
  - Alumni registration system (separate portal)
  - Event-based visiting (alumni day, fundraiser)
  - Mentorship visit tracking (alumni mentor visits student)
  - Photo permission management
  - Volunteer hour tracking

**Target Value**: Expands system use beyond parents; revenue opportunity.

## 9. INTEGRATION & API CAPABILITIES

### 9.1 Student Information System (SIS) Integration
**Use Case**: Sync student data from existing school ERP systems.
- **Features**:
  - One-way sync: Student enrollment → Visiting System
  - API endpoint for student addition/updates
  - Webhook support (when student admits, auto-create in visiting system)
  - Handles data conflicts gracefully

**Target Value**: Zero manual data entry; fits enterprise requirements.

### 9.2 CCTV/Gate Device Integration
**Use Case**: Hardware integration with school infrastructure.
- **Features**:
  - API for gate access control systems
  - CCTV timestamp matching (confirm parent entered via CCTV when QR scanned)
  - Automatic exception reports (scanned entry, but no CCTV footage)
  - Attendance syncing with time-clocks

**Target Value**: Hardware vendors become distribution partners.

### 9.3 Payment Gateway Integration
**Use Case**: Accept payments in-app.
- **Features**:
  - M-Pesa/Airtel Money (Kenya-specific)
  - Pesapal integration
  - Stripe for international payments
  - Recurring billing for membership visits
  - Automatic reconciliation

**Target Value**: Revenue stream; reduces cash handling.

### 9.4 Parental Control & KYC Verification
**Use Case**: Know-Your-Customer regulatory compliance.
- **Features**:
  - ID verification (OCR scan of national ID)
  - Biometric integration option
  - Background check API integration (with legal firms)
  - Sanctions list checking (regulatory compliance)
  - Audit trail for compliance officer review

**Target Value**: Regulatory confidence; premium market positioning.

## 10. MOBILE & OFFLINE FEATURES

### 10.1 Native Mobile Apps
**Use Case**: Parents manage visits on mobile.
- **Features**:
  - iOS & Android apps
  - Offline mode (QR code generation without internet)
  - Biometric login (fingerprint, face recognition)
  - Push notifications (visit approved instantly)
  - Photo upload capability during visits

**Target Value**: Modern UX; market expectation in mobile-first Kenya.

### 10.2 Offline Gate Checkpoint App
**Use Case**: Security checkpoint can work without constant WiFi.
- **Features**:
  - Sync when connection available
  - Pre-loaded QR code database
  - Offline photo capture (sync later)
  - Low-bandwidth mode (reduce data usage)
  - Tablet-optimized interface

**Target Value**: Works at remote school locations; reduces infrastructure costs.

### 10.3 SMS-Based Ordering & Updates
**Use Case**: For parents without smartphones.
- **Features**:
  - USSD interface (dial *123#)
  - SMS request submission ("Visit Sunday")
  - Receive approval via SMS
  - Two-way SMS updates
  - No data/internet required

**Target Value**: Accessibility; reaches broader demographic; lower churn.
